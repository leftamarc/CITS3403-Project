You will need your Steam ID to generate your SteamWrappeds

  1. Log in to Steam
  2. In the top right-hand corner, click on the drop down menu of your profile name
  3. Click on "Account details"
  4. In the top left is your username, underneath is your Steam ID
  5. Copy your Steam ID and paste it in the input field in your profile

Have more than one Steam Account?
You can save SteamWrappeds generated with different Steam IDs to a single account!
