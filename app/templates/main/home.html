SteamWrapped is an insights generating website utilising the Steam API to create "Wrappeds" personalised to your player data on Steam.

Insights galore

More insights to come...!

Gamer to gamer

This app is made by gamers, for gamers.

We have kept the gamer rhetoric in you SteamWrappeds, generate one and you'll see what we mean.

Multiple identities?
No problem!

We do not require you to link a SteamID to your account, your account on SteamWrapped is simply to store your saved Wrappeds and the Wrappeds shared with you.

You can generate from as many different SteamIDs as you want, with no limit on how many you generate at one time.

Sharing is caring

Keep and save your own Wrappeds as well as Wrappeds shared with you by other users.

Simply save a Wrapped you have generated to share.

You can even add title's to your Wrappeds to easily differentiate between them.
